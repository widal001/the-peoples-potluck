Know Your Rights trainings are one of the most accessible ways to get involved in immigrant solidarity work. These sessions teach community members about their constitutional rights during encounters with immigration enforcement, regardless of immigration status.

## What You'll Learn

- Your right to remain silent
- When and how to assert your rights
- What to do if ICE comes to your door
- How to support someone being detained
- Resources for legal assistance

## Where to Find Trainings

Several Baltimore-area organizations regularly offer Know Your Rights sessions:

- CASA de Maryland
- Immigrant Legal Resource Center
- Baltimore Immigration Court Watch

## Why This Matters

When community members know their rights, they're better equipped to protect themselves and their neighbors. This knowledge spreads through families and networks, creating a more informed and resilient community.
