Unlike criminal court, there is no right to a public defender in immigration proceedings. Yet having legal representation can mean the difference between deportation and staying with family. Legal defense funds help bridge this gap.

## Impact of Your Donation

- **5x more likely** to win their case with representation
- Average cost for basic representation: $2,000-5,000
- Your contribution joins with others to fund full case support

## Local Funds to Consider

Several organizations in the Baltimore area maintain legal defense funds:

- Maryland Immigrant Legal Defense Fund
- CASA Legal Fund
- Baltimore Immigration Bond Fund

## Other Ways to Give

Beyond direct donations:

- Set up monthly recurring contributions
- Organize workplace giving campaigns
- Host fundraising events
- Include funds in your estate planning

## Why This Matters

Access to legal counsel shouldn't depend on ability to pay. When communities pool resources, we ensure that our neighbors have a fighting chance in a system designed to be overwhelming.
