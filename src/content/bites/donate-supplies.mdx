Beyond financial donations, organizations serving immigrants often need physical supplies. Donating items can be a great way to contribute, especially if you have limited funds but can gather resources.

## Commonly Needed Items

### For Recently Released Detainees

- Clothing (especially warm layers)
- Toiletries and hygiene products
- Phone cards and prepaid phones
- Bus tickets and transit cards
- Backpacks and duffel bags

### For Families

- Diapers and baby supplies
- School supplies
- Non-perishable food
- Cleaning supplies
- Gift cards (grocery, pharmacy)

### For Organizations

- Office supplies
- Printing paper and ink
- Snacks for events
- First aid supplies

## Tips for Donating

- **Ask first**: Check what's actually needed before collecting
- **Quality matters**: Donate items you'd be happy to receive
- **Organize**: Sort and label donations for easy distribution
- **Coordinate**: Organize drives with your workplace, school, or congregation

## Why This Matters

Material support meets immediate needs and frees up organizational funds for other work. It's also an accessible way to involve people who want to help but aren't ready for other commitments.
