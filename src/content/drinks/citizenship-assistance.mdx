For those who are eligible, U.S. citizenship provides the most durable protection against deportation. Citizenship assistance programs help residents navigate the complex naturalization process.

## Services Typically Offered

- **Eligibility screening**: Determining if someone qualifies for citizenship
- **Application assistance**: Help completing the N-400 form correctly
- **Fee assistance**: Some programs cover or reduce the $725 filing fee
- **Civics preparation**: Study groups and practice tests for the citizenship exam
- **Interview coaching**: Preparation for the naturalization interview

## Why This Matters

Many eligible residents don't naturalize due to:

- Cost of application fees
- Fear of the civics and English tests
- Confusion about eligibility requirements
- Lack of time to navigate the process

## What Baltimore Thirsts For

Expanded citizenship assistance with:

- More locations and flexible scheduling
- Fee assistance programs
- Multilingual staff and materials
- Follow-up support through the entire process

## Current Resources

Several organizations in Baltimore offer citizenship assistance. Wait times can be long, highlighting the need for expanded capacity.
