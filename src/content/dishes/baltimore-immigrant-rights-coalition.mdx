The Baltimore Immigrant Rights Coalition brings together community organizations, faith groups, legal services providers, and advocates to coordinate efforts for immigrant justice in Baltimore.

## Coalition Work

- **Policy advocacy**: Working with city and state officials on pro-immigrant policies
- **Know Your Rights**: Community education on rights during ICE encounters
- **Rapid response coordination**: Coordinating community response to ICE activity
- **Resource sharing**: Connecting community members with legal, social, and emergency services

## Member Organizations

The coalition includes representatives from legal aid organizations, churches, community centers, and grassroots groups across the city.
