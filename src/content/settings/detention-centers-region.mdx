Understanding where detention facilities are located helps families find their loved ones and informs advocacy efforts to end immigration detention.

## Regional Facilities

People arrested by ICE in Baltimore may be held at several facilities:

- **Howard County Detention Center**: Local jail with ICE contract
- **Worcester County Jail**: Eastern Shore facility
- **Facilities in Virginia, Pennsylvania, and beyond**: People are often transferred far from their communities

## The Business of Detention

Immigration detention is a multi-billion dollar industry. Private prison companies profit from contracts to detain immigrants, creating financial incentives to maintain and expand detention.

## Impact on Families

When someone is detained:

- Families may not know where they are for days
- Visitation is difficult, especially at distant facilities
- Legal representation is harder to access
- Children may be left without caregivers

## Organizing Against Detention

Community groups work to end detention contracts, support detained individuals, and advocate for alternatives to detention.
