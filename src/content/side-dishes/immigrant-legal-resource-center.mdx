Access to immigration legal services can mean the difference between staying with family or facing deportation. Local legal resource centers provide crucial support for community members navigating the complex immigration system.

## Services Offered

- **Naturalization assistance**: Help with citizenship applications and test preparation
- **DACA renewals**: Support for Dreamers maintaining their status
- **Family petitions**: Help reuniting families through legal immigration channels
- **Removal defense**: Representation for those facing deportation proceedings

## How to Access Services

Most centers offer free consultations and sliding-scale fees based on income. Call ahead to schedule an appointment and bring all relevant documents.
