CASA de Maryland is one of the largest immigrant advocacy organizations in the mid-Atlantic region. They provide direct services including legal assistance, workforce development, health services, and civic engagement programs.

## What They Do

- **Legal Services**: Immigration legal assistance and know-your-rights trainings
- **Workforce Development**: Job training and placement programs
- **Health Programs**: Connecting community members with healthcare resources
- **Advocacy**: Fighting for immigrant-friendly policies at local, state, and federal levels

Visit their website to learn about volunteer opportunities, donate, or access their services.
